Phase 01.01: Prepare for Risk Assessment

    Next

The first step in the risk assessment process is to prepare for the assessment. The aim of this phase is to establish a context for the risk assessment [2].

Preparing for risk assessment is comprised of the following tasks


Task 01: – Identify the purpose of the assessment

Task 02: – Identify the scope of the assessment

Task 03: - Identify the assumptions and constraints associated with the assessment

Task 04: - Identify the sources of information to be used as inputs to the assessment

Task 05: – Identify the risk model and analytical approach
