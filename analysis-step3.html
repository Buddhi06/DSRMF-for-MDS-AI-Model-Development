Phase 1: Step 1:
Activity 2 - Identification of assets, threats, and vulnerabilities

Identification of assets, threats, and vulnerabilities

To identify the assets, threats, and vulnerabilities, this framework recommends following the Threat Modeling (TM) process below:

# Phase Description
1 Objectives Identification Identify the security properties that the MDS AI model should have.
2 Survey Identify the stages of the AI model development lifecycle.
3 Asset Identification Identify the assets at each stage of the AI model development lifecycle that could be compromised by the threats.
4 Threat Identification Identify the threats to the assets that may cause it to fail to achieve security objectives identified in phase 01.
5 Vulnerability Identification Examine the identified threats and identify if the MDS AI model is vulnerable with respect to the identified threats.
