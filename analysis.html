Guidance on Performing Risk Analysis

    Back        Next

Risk analysis is the process of comprehending the types of risk and determining the level of risks.

Risk analysis is comprised of the following steps


Step 1: Identify the intended use and the characteristics related to data security

Step 2: Identification of assets, threats, vulnerabilities and adverse impact

Step 3: Estimation of risk(s) for each applicable threat and vulnerability combination
