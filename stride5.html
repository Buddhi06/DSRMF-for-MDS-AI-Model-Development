Step 05: Vulnerability Identification

    Next

The last step of the STRIDE threat modelling is to identify the vulnerabilities associated with the threats identified in step 04. A vulnerability is “a weakness in an information system, system security procedure, internal controls, or implementation that could be exploited or triggered by a threat source” [1].
