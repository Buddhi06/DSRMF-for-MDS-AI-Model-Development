A Risk Management Process for assuring Data Security when developing MDS AI Models

The purpose of this framework is to provide a comprehensive guidance on performing a risk management process for addressing the data security risks that arise during MDS AI model development and assure that the data remains secure

The phases of the risk management process provided in this framework correspond those outlined in AAMI TIR 57 - Principles for Medical Device Security - Risk Management

The scope of this framework encompasses the "entire MDS AI Model Development Lifecycle"

Phase 1: Risk Assessment

The purpose of this phase is to - "establish a baseline assessment of data security risks in MDS AI model development by identifying assets, threats, and vulnerabilities, estimating risks and evaluating the identified risks".
